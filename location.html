Cities in kenya

Mombasa

The white and the blue city

Mombasa is a coastal city in southern kenya along the Indian ocean.It was the first capital of British East Arica,before Nairobi was elevated to caitl city status in 1907.


Nairobi

The green city in the sun

Nairobi is situated at in South -Central Kenya,140 kilometres (87miles)south of the equator.It is surrounded by plains , cliffs and forest that makes up the city's Nairobi National Park.
